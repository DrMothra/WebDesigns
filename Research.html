Research

My main research interests centre around three-dimensional computer graphics, in the form of visualisations, simulation, virtual environments and graphical tools. Currently I am developing a number of applications to visualise and manipulate data over the web. These make use of emerging technologies that allow for rendering of complex graphical data (both 2D and 3D) and realtime interaction in the browser. Check out the projects and portfolio sections to see examples of the work. If you would like to collaborate on a project then please get in touch.
