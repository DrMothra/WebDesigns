Research

My main research interests centre around three-dimensional computer graphics, in the form of visualisations, simulation, virtual environments and graphical tools. Currently I am developing a number of applications to visualise and manipulate data over the web. These make use of emerging technologies that allow for rendering of complex graphical data (both 2D and 3D) and realtime interaction in the browser.
